Welcome Desk

Located at the East (Main) entrance of the Students’ Union building, the welcome desk is there to help with any problems that you may have. If you are lost or don’t know where to get the help you need, the welcome desk is the place to go. The welcome desk also deals with lost property so if you think you have lost an item in the SU then it may be located at the welcome desk.

  • Monday - Friday: 8.30am-8.45pm
  • Saturday: 9am-8.45pm
  • Sunday: 12pm-8.45pm
