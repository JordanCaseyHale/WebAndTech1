INTERVAL Kitchen & Bar

Interval serves a selection of draft beers, fine wine and tasty food as a reasonable price. They also have a lovely atmosphere and a beer garden which is fantastic for a hot Summers day. There are also live DJs, open mic nights and jazz nights for when you fancy some music.
