Box Office

The box office is the place to go to get all your tickets for Student Union run activities and events. Alternatively you can go on the website here to buy your tickets and to see upcoming events.

  • Opening Times:
  • Monday, Thursday & Friday: 11am-6pm (General sales)
  • Tuesday: 11am-6pm (General sales) 11pm-12.45am (Club night only)
  • Wednesday: 11am-6pm (General sales) 11.30pm-12.45am (Club night only)
  • Saturday: 12.30pm-5pm (General sales), 11.30pm-1.15am (Club night only)
  • Sunday: 7pm-9.30pm (Hawklords gig only)

Sports & Activities Zone

If you are interested in any extracurricular activities but have no idea where to start, pop over to the Welcome Desk, and they will be able to give you information on GIAG events,sports and sheffield volunteering. It's also a great space for socialising and studying.

  • Opening Times for Sports & Activities Zone:
  • Monday - Friday: 8.30am - 11pm
  • Saturday: 9am-11pm
  • Sunday: 12noon-11pm
  • Opening Times for Sports & Activities Zone Welcome Desk:
  • Monday - Friday: 10am - 6pm
  • Saturday - Sunday: Closed
