Bar One

The best place to be to have a cheeky pint after a long day of uni, or if you just fancy a nasty tower burger for lunch and catch up with your friends. Karaoke Nights every Thursday and Quiz Nights every Sunday, perfect to take all your uni stress away!

  • Link: http://bit.ly/2FsNFTd
  • Link for Bar One Facebook page: http://bit.ly/2FhTUce
  • Opening Times:
  • Monday: 11am - 12am (food served from 11am - 9pm)
  • Tuesday - Wednesday: 11am - 12.30am (food served from 11am - 9pm)
  • Thursday: 11am - 1.30am (food served from 11am - 9pm) Yo Karaoke
  • Friday - Saturday: 11am - 1am (food served from 11am - 9pm)
  • Sunday: 12noon - 12am (food served from 12am - 9pm)

Foundry

Foundry is a night club on the second floor of the Students' Union Building, famous night outs include Roar on Wednesdays and Pop Tarts on Saturdays. Student can use their students' ID as identification, and students can buy their tickets either on the Foundry website, or at the Box Office in person.

  • Link: https://foundrysu.com/
  • Link for Bar One Facebook page: https://www.facebook.com/Foundrysu/
